OTTER

Open Toolkit for Training in Education Research

OTTER is a graduate-student-led research methods learning collective at The Ohio State University. Each month, one student takes the lead on a research method — three weeks of study together, one public talk open to all. The methods we explore, and the materials we produce, live here for the community.

How It Works

A monthly cycle.

  1. 01

    Group study

    Weeks 1–3 — the monthly leader picks a method, shares one or two readings, and guides three weeks of small-group discussion within EED.

  2. 02

    Public talk

    Week 4 — the leader gives an open talk on the method to all OSU students. In person, not recorded.

  3. 03

    Open materials

    Slides, readings, and notes from every talk live on this site, free for anyone to revisit or adapt.
